The Process

From weak profile to
dominant reputation

We handle everything. You focus on your patients.

01
Free Reputation Audit

We analyse your current Google profile, review count, rating, and how you compare to local competitors. You get a clear picture of exactly what's costing you patients.

02
Profile Optimisation

We fully optimise your Google Business Profile — photos, descriptions, services, Q&A, and posts. Designed to rank higher and convert more searchers into bookings.

03
Review Generation

We set up automated review requests sent to patients at exactly the right moment. Most clients see 15–25 new 5-star reviews per month within 60 days.

04
Review Responses

Every review — good or bad — gets a professional, personalised response written in your brand voice. This signals trust to both patients and Google's algorithm.

05
Ongoing Monitoring

We monitor your reputation across Google, Facebook, and Trustpilot. Any negative review is flagged and handled immediately — protecting what you've built.

06
Monthly Reporting

Every month you receive a clear report showing your rating progress, review volume, competitor comparison, and what we're focusing on next.
